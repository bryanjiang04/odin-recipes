Steak

This is the famous steak cooked by Chef Bryan. This steak dish is first coated heavily in salt and pepper. It's then scorched in an oven for 3 minutes at 350 degrees Fahrenheit.

The ingredients are simple: a steak, some seasoning, and a whole lotta love!

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Heat oven
  2. Season steaks heavily
  3. Add butter to baste
